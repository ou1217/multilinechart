Commute Time to Work in Missouri Cities:2005-2013

By Sifan Ouyang
Published March 8th, 2015

Statistics showed the average commute time in Columbia, Mo., has been one of the lowest in all Missouri cities surveyed by the U.S. Census Bureau from 2005 to 2013. From 2011 to 2013, the average commute time for Columbia residents dropped from the highest (20.2 min) to the lowest point (16.6 min) of the nine-year period.

According to the American Community Survey, the estimated commute time in a city came from a sample of local residents who are older than 16 years old and work outside their homes. Seven metropolitan and micropolitan areas, including Columbia, served as sampling locations in Missouri. Other locations are Branson, MO Micro Area, Cape Girardeau, MO-IL Metro Area, Farmington, MO Micro Area,Fayetteville-Springdale-Rogers, AR-MO Metro Area,Jefferson City, MO Metro Area,Joplin, MO Metro Area, and Kansas City, MO-KS Metro Area.
